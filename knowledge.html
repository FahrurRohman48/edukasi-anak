Animal Knowledge

Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. They live in groups called prides, which are family units that may include up to three males, a dozen or so females, and their young.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, social behaviors, and strong family bonds. They are also known for their long trunks, which they use for various tasks.

Dolphin

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and complex social structures. They are found in oceans and rivers around the world.

Panda

Pandas are black and white bears known for their distinctive markings and bamboo diet. They are native to China and are considered a national treasure.
